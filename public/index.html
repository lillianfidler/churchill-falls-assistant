Churchill Falls Information Assistant

Empowering through information

This assistant provides expert analysis and comprehensive information about Churchill Falls hydroelectric projects and energy agreements between Newfoundland and Labrador and Quebec. Ask questions using voice or text, and choose between quick summaries or in-depth research.

Voice
Hear Dr. Doug May's analysis in his own voice. Press and hold the microphone button below to speak your question.
Fast
Fast, focused answers using core documents and expert analysis, without deep historical detail. Type your question below.
Deep
Comprehensive research with text answers from all sources (view sources). Type your question below.

All responses are based exclusively on verified source documents. Every answer draws from official documents including the December 12, 2024 MOU, Dr. Doug May's comprehensive analysis, expert economic assessments by Wade Locke and James Feehan, Churchill Falls Corporation financial statements, and historical records dating to 1949. This assistant does not use internet searches or external sources—only curated materials from the Sources page.
