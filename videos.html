About Dr. Doug May

Dr. Doug May is Professor Emeritus in the Department of Economics at Memorial University of Newfoundland and Labrador. His research focuses on energy economics, regional economic development, and public policy analysis.

His video series on the December 2024 Churchill Falls MOU represents the most comprehensive independent economic assessment of the proposed agreement. Dr. May's methodology emphasizes transparency, accessibility, and balanced analysis while acknowledging the limitations of publicly available information.

Note: All of Dr. May's video analyses and written documents are integrated into the Churchill Falls Information Assistant's knowledge base. You can ask questions about any aspect of his analysis through the chat interface.
