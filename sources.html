Dr. Doug May's Video Analysis Series

Dr. Doug May is Professor Emeritus in the Department of Economics at Memorial University of Newfoundland and Labrador. His comprehensive 6-part video series provides an independent economic assessment of the December 2024 Churchill Falls MOU.

Note: All of Dr. May's video analyses and written documents are integrated into the assistant's knowledge base. You can ask questions about any aspect of his analysis through the chat interface.

Video 1: Quebec's Emerging Electricity Shortage

Analysis of Quebec's electricity supply challenges and the context for the proposed MOU.

Video 2A: Electricity Pricing - Quebec's Costs

Examination of Quebec's electricity cost structure and pricing considerations.

Video 2B: Electricity Pricing - Churchill Falls' Role

Analysis of Churchill Falls' position in electricity pricing discussions.

Video 3A: Hydro-Québec's Electricity Imports

Review of Hydro-Québec's electricity import patterns and implications.

Video 3B: Hydro-Québec's Electricity Exports

Analysis of Hydro-Québec's export market and revenue generation.

Video 4: Water Royalties and Resource Rents

Examination of water royalty structures and resource rent considerations.
