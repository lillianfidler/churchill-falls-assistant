FINANCIAL RESULTS 2024

REVENUE

Total revenue in 2024 was $195,496 thousand ($195.5 million), up from $164,573 thousand ($164.6 million) in 2023, an increase of $30,923 thousand or 18.8%.

Revenue breaks down as follows for 2024: energy sales at $125,542 thousand (64.2% of total revenue), guaranteed winter availability at $38,293 thousand (19.6%), other revenue at $22,615 thousand (11.6%), and net finance income at $9,046 thousand (4.6%).

For comparison, 2023 revenue was: energy sales at $113,070 thousand (68.7%), guaranteed winter availability at $38,897 thousand (23.6%), net finance income at $8,964 thousand (5.4%), and other revenue at $3,642 thousand (2.2%).

Year-over-year changes: energy sales increased $12,472 thousand or 11.0%, guaranteed winter availability decreased $604 thousand or 1.6%, other revenue increased $18,973 thousand or 520.9%, and net finance income increased $82 thousand or 0.9%.

PROFIT

Profit for the year 2024 was $81,533 thousand ($81.5 million), up from $54,099 thousand ($54.1 million) in 2023, an increase of $27,434 thousand or 50.7%. Total comprehensive income was $83,065 thousand in 2024 compared to $52,462 thousand in 2023, an increase of $30,603 thousand or 58.3%.

EXPENSES

Total expenses in 2024 were $113,963 thousand, up from $110,474 thousand in 2023, an increase of $3,489 thousand or 3.2%. The breakdown for 2024: operating costs at $70,798 thousand (62.1% of total expenses), depreciation and amortization at $30,640 thousand (26.9%), and other expense at $12,525 thousand (11.0%).

Year-over-year changes: operating costs increased $125 thousand or 0.2%, depreciation and amortization decreased $6,783 thousand or 18.1%, and other expense increased $10,147 thousand or 426.7%.

Specific operating costs from the notes include salaries and benefits over $43 million, maintenance at $12.7 million, and water royalties at $8.79 million.

---

BALANCE SHEET AS AT DECEMBER 31 2024

Total assets in 2024 were $1,294,705 thousand ($1.29 billion), up from $1,203,187 thousand ($1.20 billion) in 2023, an increase of $91,518 thousand or 7.6%.

Current assets in 2024 totalled $234,234 thousand comprising cash at $138,670 thousand, trade and other receivables at $51,630 thousand, inventories at $22,970 thousand, current portion of reserve fund at $18,236 thousand, restricted cash at $298 thousand, and prepayments at $2,430 thousand.

Non-current assets in 2024 comprised property, plant and equipment at $1,002,582 thousand (77.4% of total assets), reserve fund at $57,135 thousand, and intangible assets at $754 thousand.

Total liabilities in 2024 were $177,698 thousand and total equity was $1,117,007 thousand. Current liabilities of $69,963 thousand comprised trade and other payables at $51,675 thousand, rental and royalty payable at $8,679 thousand, deferred revenue at $6,112 thousand, current portion of decommissioning and environmental liabilities at $1,486 thousand, current portion of deferred credits at $1,514 thousand, and current portion of deferred contributions at $497 thousand. Non-current liabilities were $107,735 thousand.

---

KEY FINANCIAL RATIOS

Profit margin in 2024 was 41.7% (profit $81.5 million divided by revenue $195.5 million), up from 32.9% in 2023. Equity ratio was 86.3% (equity $1,117 million divided by total assets $1,295 million), up from 85.9% in 2023. Current ratio was 3.35 (current assets $234 million divided by current liabilities $70 million), up from 2.83 in 2023.

---

YEAR-OVER-YEAR CHANGES

Cash holdings increased $7,945 thousand or 6.1%. Receivables increased $25,804 thousand or 99.9%. Property, plant and equipment increased $57,095 thousand or 6.0%.

---

WATER ROYALTIES

Payment to the NL government in 2024 was $8,790 thousand ($8.79 million) at a rate of 0.025 cents per kWh paid to the Government of Newfoundland and Labrador.
