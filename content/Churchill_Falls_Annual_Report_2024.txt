## MOU CONTEXT

### Current Financial Performance

**This 2024 report represents the LAST full year** before MOU pricing takes effect (Jan 1, 2025)

### Historical Context

**2024 represents:**
- Last year of 1969 contract pricing (0.2¢/kWh)
- Baseline for measuring MOU impact
- Final year of GWAC at current terms

### Expected Changes Under MOU

**Starting 2025:**
- Price increases from 0.2¢/kWh to 5.9¢/kWh (30× increase)
- GWAC to be replaced
- Revenue expected to increase dramatically

**Projected annual revenue to CF(L)Co under MOU:** ~$1.17 billion (average to 2041)
- Compared to 2024 revenue: $195.5 million
- **Expected increase:** ~6× current revenue

**NL treasury share (65.8%):** ~$768 million annually
- Compared to 2024 NL share: ~$53.6 million
- **Expected increase:** ~14× current NL return
