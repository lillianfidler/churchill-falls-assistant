About Dr. Doug May

Dr. Doug May is an economist and expert on Churchill Falls energy policy. His analysis provides critical insights into the economic and political dimensions of the Churchill Falls agreements, including detailed cost comparisons, revenue analysis, and strategic implications for Newfoundland and Labrador.

How to Use

Choose your preferred interaction method:

  • Voice: Press and hold the microphone button to speak your question. Release to hear Dr. May's analysis.
  • Text (Fast): Type your question and select Fast mode for a concise answer.
